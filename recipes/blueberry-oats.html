Bluebery Oats

Description

Oats with blueberries! Also has milk. Recipe is from allrecipes

Ingredients (1 Serving)

  • 1/2 cup quick-cooking oats
  • 1 tablespoon chopped walnuts
  • 1 teaspoon ground flax
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1/4 cup frozen blueberries, thawed

Steps

  1. Mix oats, walnuts, brown sugar, flax, chia seeds, sesame seeds, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Pour boiling water over the oats mixture and stir so ingredients are completely moistened
  3. steep until the oats are softened, about 2 minutes
  4. Stir blueberries through the oats mixture
