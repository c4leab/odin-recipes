Scrabbled-eggs

Classic Scrambled eggs.

Ingredients (1 Serving)

  • 2 eggs
  • pinch of salt
  • seasoning of your choice
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. whisk together eggs in a bowl
  2. Preheat pan to medium temperature
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil onto pan; spread the oil around the pan
  4. pour the oil on the pan
  5. pour eggs on the oiled up pan
  6. scramble the eggs on the pan until you get the consistency you want
  7. sprinkle some salt and seasoning of your choice while stirring
  8. Once ready, put it into a plate and eat it by itself or with a side of your choice
