Toasted Turkey Sandwhich

Description

Delicious Turkey Sandwhich with toasted bread.

Ingredients (1 Serving)

  • 2 slices of bread
  • handful of lettuce
  • 1 bell-pepper
  • 6 slices of oven-baked turkey
  • Chipotle/Jalepeno sauce

Steps

  1. Put both slices of bread in a toaster
  2. Cut the lettuce into small chunks
  3. Cut the Bell peppers into small chunks
  4. Heatup turkey slices in microwave for 30 seconds
  5. Once toasted, put Sauce of your choosing on bread
  6. Stack the rest of the ingredients in between bread slices
  7. [Optional] Slice sandwhich into triangles
